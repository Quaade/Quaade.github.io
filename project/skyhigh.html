Skyhigh Saboteur
Exploring Depth in Incremental Games

2020
Incremental RPG
Solo


Made for the 2020 GMTK Game Jam, which tasked designers to make a game based on the theme "Out of Control" Players take control of a small guy in a parachute, with the goal of infiltrating enemy planes and blowing them up, direction them towards crashing into as many other planes as possible before the time runs out, creating a unique twist on games like Peggle or Pachinko
