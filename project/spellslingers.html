Spell Slingers
Twin-Stick ringout shooter

2013
Roguelite Twin-Stick Shooter
Solo


A quick one-week project made on a laptop in a hotel room. Up to 4 players on a mix of keyboard and controllers try to knock each other out on an ever shrinking arena. The game has each player pick from one of 4 different characters, each with 4 unique abilities to either launch the opponent out, or prevent the opponent from doing the same to them. Not quite a looker, but quite fun!
