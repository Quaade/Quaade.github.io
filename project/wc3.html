Mods and other hobby projects
and many, many Warcraft maps

2006-2015
Design
Solo


Growing up, i spent plenty of nights in a living room crowded with sleeping bags and PC's with my childhood friends, playing Warcraft 3 mods into the morning. So I quickly got interested in how to make some of my own, which kicked off my interest in gamedev.

several MOBA's, weird experimental concepts, and at one point trying to force the Warcraft engine to work as a first person shooter. At this point i am not quite sure how many different Warcraft maps. Somewhere between 15 and 20. Some of them did gather a small following on Battle.net although i think many of them are lost to time, so you'll just have to take my word for it.

After getting started with Warcraft maps, i looked into making my own standalone games. My first projects being simple 2D platformers or Shoot-em-up's made in Game Maker. I've since then moved on from Game Maker, although it does hold a special place in my heart.
