Warcraft 3 Maps
Many, many Warcraft maps

2006-2012
Design
Solo


Growing up, i spent plenty of nights in a living room crowded with sleeping bags and PC's with my childhood friends, playing Warcraft 3 mods into the morning. I quickly got interested in how to make some of my own, and from there how to make stand alone games.

At this point i am not quite sure how many different Warcraft maps. Somewhere between 15 and 20 i would assume. Some of them did garner small communities on Blizzards servers, although i think many of them are lost to time, so you'll just have to take my word for it.
