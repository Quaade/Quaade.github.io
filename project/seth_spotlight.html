Design Spotlight


The above character spotlight can serve as a quick visual example of some of the work I’ve done on Rushdown Revolt.

I was directly responsible for design and implementation for the bulk of moves, mechanics, VFX, and systems showcased in this video, as well as similar content across the rest of the cast. This involved direct participation in all steps of the process, including:
  • Top level design and analysis.
  • Concepting and ideation.
  • In-editor implementation of character moves and properties.
  • Engine programming for new features and systems.
  • VFX design and implementation.
  • Live balance and iteration.
